What is 'Youth Empowerment Seminar PLUS'?!

The highly demanding schedules of college students and young professionals leave them overprogramed and overwhelmed. For those individuals that wish to make a difference in our global community, this is particulary an issue: their highly active lifestyles leave them too occupied to know where to begin.

Based on the ancient yogic sciences of breath, physical posture, and meditation... as well as simple, practical wisdom, the YES!+ Workshop seeks to both educate and empower participants to "Be the Change"! Join college students and young professionals from around the country as they discover 'their inner leader' in a 6 day adventure of rejuvenative growth!
